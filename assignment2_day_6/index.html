Welcome to the demo

This page shows how a simple dark mode toggle can transform the reading experience. Use the button in the top-right to switch themes.

Why dark mode?

Many people prefer a dark background in low light or at night. It can reduce eye strain and save energy on OLED screens.

Reduced eye strain

Softer contrast in dim environments can make long reading sessions more comfortable.

Better for OLED

True black pixels stay off, which can extend battery life on phones and laptops.

Your preference, saved

The theme you choose will stay even after refreshing the page.
